What's the annual leave policy for field operations staff?
2:31 PM

Based on HR Policy Manual, Rotational Leave Policy 2024

Based on Seepco's HR Policy Manual (Section 4.2), field operations staff are entitled to:

  • 21 working days of annual leave per calendar year
  • Rotational staff follow a 28-day field / 28-day leave cycle under the Rotational Leave Policy
  • Unused leave must be taken within 6 months of the following year or it lapses

Leave requests should be submitted via the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance and require line manager approval before processing.

How many days can I carry over to next year?
2:34 PM

Based on Leave Management SOP §3.1, HR Policy Manual §4.5

You can carry over a maximum of 10 working days from the previous year. These must be used within the first 6 months of the new leave year — by June 30th — or they will be forfeited.

For exceptional circumstances such as project criticality or extended medical leave, your line manager can submit a carry-over extension request to HR. This requires VP-level approval and must be submitted before December 15th of the current leave year.
